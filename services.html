Services in Details

Routine Service (Charges include charges for Pick n' drop and Doorstep)

  • Moped 100cc-125cc : Service: Rs 399
  • Bike 100cc-125cc : Service: Rs 299
  • Bike 135cc-180cc : Service: Rs 399
  • Bike 180cc-220cc : Service: Rs 499
  • Bike 250cc-400cc : Service: Rs 649
  • RE 350cc-500cc : Service: Rs 699

( Charges are excluding engine oil )



Routine Service includes :

  • Air Filter Cleaning
  • Spark Plug Cleaning
  • Washing
  • Greasing/ Oiling
  • Engine oil replacement/ check
  • Clutch Cable inspect/ setting
  • Brake Cable inspect/ setting
  • Carburettor setting/ drain
  • Chain adjustment setting
  • All lights check [Headlight, tail lamp, indicator]
  • Accelerator Cable inspect/ setting
  • Front & rear disc pad cleaning/ setting
  • Disc brake oil top up if required
  • Minor Fitting / nut bolts replacements

Breakdown Assistance Bike:


Emergency Breakdown: Charges depend on the severity of the issue.

  • Minor Issue: (Emergency Visiting Charges Rs 199) + (Charges for problem solving)
  • E.G : Minor Issue: Clutch Cable breakdown – Charges will be (Visiting Charges Rs 199 + Clutch Cable RP + Clutch Cable replacement labour)


Minor Issue Type :

  1. Spare wear & tear: Visiting Charges Rs 199 + Spare MRP + Spare replacement labour)
  2. Flat tyre: Visiting Charges Rs 199 + Tyre replacement labour + Cost for puncture removal)
  3. Petrol: (Visiting Charges Rs 199 + 2Litre Petrol Cost)
  4. Issue Unknown: Rs 199, If any issue is not able to get identified on the spot the mechanic will pickup the bike to the workshop, in this case pickup charges will be applicable


Major issue type: Only Transportation Charges

  1. Emergency Accidental breakdown: Rs 1000 – Rs 4000 ( Depending on the condition of bike and time of service)
  2. Scheduled Breakdown: Rs 500 – Rs 1500
